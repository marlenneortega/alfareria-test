Pottery is a representative activity of our municipality, San Felipe Guanajuato, Mexico. This is because of the mud quality. It is also due to the historic context that still remains in our roots. However, over the years, this activity has become less common and just a few people are engaged in the sale and production of pottery. Some have decided to definitely stop the elaboration, and simply dedicate to another activity. Our purpose with this project, is to increase the sale of pottery objects, in order to support this traditional practice and avoid its disappearance.
